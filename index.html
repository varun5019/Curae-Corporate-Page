Curaae

Curae is a revolutionary app that would become a staple for the medical sector of our country. With abundant amenities for patients, doctors and pharmaceuticals as well, Curae aims to be the number one go-to app for all your medical tasks.

Curae strives to help integrate doctors, patients, hospitals and diagnostic centres on just one platform, for convenient procuring and handling of records, and booking of appointments. With an easy user interface and handling, the app has been designed to be of maximum help to the elderly as well.

Seamless User Interface

Curae has a convenient user interface designed to cater to everyone from young to old. With icons and labels that are easy to identify and understand, the app aims to enable everyone to access its superior functionalities. The ergonomic navigation bar holds all the pages that one might want to access.

Conviniently schedule appointments

Patients can easily book their appointments and cancel anytime they want to, with just a few clicks. The appointments tab shows the dates and timeslots available, and you can choose as per your convenience.

Accept and manage appointments

Doctors can accept appointments and manage them by accessing the appointments tab in their profile. It also allows them to enter the timings that they're comfortable with for consultations, so that patients can book their slots in those hours.
